FAQ

Quick answers for beginners.

Are the templates really no-code?

Yes—templates are designed for drag-and-drop editing. You can still add custom code later if you want, but it’s optional.

What does “100% customizable” mean?

You can edit layout sections, colors, typography, images, and page content. If you need deeper changes, our pros can help.

Can I accept payments through Stripe?

Yes. You can connect Stripe to sell products, accept deposits, or charge for services. A simple backend endpoint is required for live Checkout.

Do you offer custom builds?

Yes—custom builds start at $250 and go up to $2,000 depending on pages, features, and ecommerce requirements.
